Indian Institute Of Information Technology, Ranchi

Everyone has some expectations while coming into a college, they prepare for the entrance exam with their full might and then select a college according to their needs and choices. Thus everyone has somewhat a few expectations from their colleges. According to their level of expectation they are hit hard when these turn into a totally different reality :)

EXPECTATIONS

  • Campus would be spread in acres as seen in videos of IITs and NITs

  • Great Sports Facilities with huge grounds and proper equipments.
  • No Homework and stress free life.
  • No Issue of attendance and mass bunks every day
  • Poor Mess food which would hardly go down our throat and confuse our livers.
  • Staying away from home and totally relaxing without anyone to scold.
  • A decent 60:40 gender ratio which is normal in national colleges.
  • Very supportive teachers who would easliy help us in passing the semester exams and preventing backlogs.
  • Fests, Parties and clubbing.

    • REALITY

  • No permanent campus and the temporary campus is also just a combination of 6 floored buildings.
  • No proper sporting ground, just a single uneven ground that too just for the freshers and no badminton court or football ground.
  • Each subject has 2,3 due assignments.
  • 80% attendance strictly required for sitting in exams.
  • Mess food surprised me the most, with such a variety everyday and quallity is also good.
  • Never felt attached to home like this and initial days were tough to handle.
  • Every where I look even in the class I see boys :)
  • Our only messiah is youtube and that too Gajendra Purohit sir.
  • Mid Sem Exams, End Sem Exams, Surprise tests every 1 to 2 months which require daily revision so I attend fests in my dreams.
